About Me

I am Freya Sostack, based in Odense, Denmark. I'm studying multimeadia design fokusing on Web design, app design and branding. Passionate about designing a memorable digital experiences, I love bringing a unique but simple and minimalistic touch to the design in every project. I focus on creating engaging websites with unique layouts, captivating interactions, and a strong emphasis on typography. Detail-oriented, innovative, and client-focused, I thrive on enhancing user experiences. Beyond design, I enjoy painting, gardening, and strength training. Let's create your dream project together!
